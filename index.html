OUR WORK

Fan Instillation

We recently completed a ceiling fan installation project to improve air circulation and energy efficiency in the home. By replacing outdated fixtures with modern ceiling fans, we not only enhanced the comfort of the living space but also reduced reliance on air conditioning, leading to potential energy savings. The installation process involved careful wiring and mounting to ensure optimal performance and safety for the homeowners.

Bathroom Remodel

During the bathroom remodel project, we started by dismantling the outdated fixtures and stripping away the worn tiles. With meticulous care, we removed the old shower, revealing the underlying plumbing for inspection and potential upgrades. This initial phase laid the foundation for a fresh, modern bathroom space tailored to the client's preferences and needs.

Landscaping

During the landscaping project, we undertook the task of constructing a stunning brick wall to delineate and enhance the outdoor space. Collaborating closely with the client, we meticulously selected plants and shrubbery that complemented the rustic charm of the brickwork, bringing their envisioned garden to life with a blend of texture and color.

Pressure Washing

We revitalized the exterior of the property through a thorough pressure washing session, stripping away years of grime and dirt buildup to reveal its original vibrancy. The high-pressure cleaning technique breathed new life into the surfaces.

Kitchen Cabinets

We began by removing the old cabinets and carefully selecting custom lighting to enhance the kitchen's atmosphere. Every detail, from material choice to cabinet design, was meticulously considered to achieve a modern and personalized space. The result was a stunning kitchen renovation that seamlessly combined functionality and style, exceeding the client's expectations.

Installed Rain Gutters

We undertook a rain gutter installation project to address water drainage issues around the property. By strategically placing gutters along the roofline, we effectively directed rainwater away from the foundation, preventing potential water damage and erosion.
